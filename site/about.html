About the Project

Millions of people a year tune in to watch NBA games to see their favorite athletes perform. What if they could watch themselves slam those dunks and drain those game-winning shots? With Everybody DUNK Now, users can simply upload a video of themselves moving around and then be transformed into the next Stephen Curry. After running a pose detector on the submitted video and user-selected video of an NBA player, a trained Generative Adversarial Network (“GAN”) maps the user’s body and face onto the basketball player. Just like that the user is shown dunking!
